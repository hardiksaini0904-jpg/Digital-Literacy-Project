Stay Safe Online – Prevention Tips:


1. Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone
2. Do not scan unknown QR codes
3. Always verify payment requests before accepting
4. Avoid clicking on suspicious links
5. Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication
6. Keep your apps and devices updated
7. Do not share personal or banking details online
8. Be cautious of unknown calls claiming to be from banks

UPI Safety Tips:
9. Remember that receiving money does not require entering PIN
10. Always check the receiver details before making payment
